About ComplaintCA

Last updated: July 21, 2026

ComplaintCA is a Canadian civic complaint platform, built to help people reach the correct authority for their issue — without guesswork, and without needing a lawyer for most of it.

What ComplaintCA Is

ComplaintCA routes complaints, legal-rights claims, and access-to-information requests to the correct Canadian authority — municipal, provincial, or federal — based on what actually happened. It offers AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel for cases that need real representation, and cyber crime reporting support.

It's built and maintained by an independent founder, not a law firm or government body. The guides on this site are cross-checked against the official authority named in each one, and updated when that information changes.

How We Verify Information

Real authorities, not AI guesses

Every authority, deadline, and legal citation shown to a user is drawn from a manually verified reference table — not generated freely by the AI. If a specific detail isn't confirmed, the system is instructed to say so rather than invent one.

Sources you can check yourself

Every guide links directly to the official government, tribunal, or ombudsman page it references, so you can verify it independently rather than take our word for it.

What ComplaintCA Is Not

ComplaintCA is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or representation. AI-drafted content is a starting point to review, not a finished legal document. For matters requiring representation, the Lawyer Panel connects you with independent legal counsel — ComplaintCA itself does not practice law.
