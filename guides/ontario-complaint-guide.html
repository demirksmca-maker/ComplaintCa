How to File a Complaint in Ontario

Where you file depends on what happened. This guide sorts the most common complaint types in Ontario by the authority responsible — and shows where ComplaintCA fits in to help you get there faster, in your own language.

Frequently Asked

Where do I file a consumer complaint in Ontario?

Consumer Protection Ontario, online or at 1-800-889-9768.

Where do I complain about a municipal service in Toronto?

Start with the relevant city department; if unresolved, escalate to Ombudsman Toronto.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.

How do I complain to my MP?

Use ComplaintCA's MP Finder to locate your riding's representative and draft a formal letter.

Where do I report cyber crime in Canada?

The Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre and your local police service. ComplaintCA's Cyber Crime module helps you document the report first.
