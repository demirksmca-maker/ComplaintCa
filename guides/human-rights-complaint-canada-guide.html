Frequently Asked

Where do I file a human rights complaint against a federal employer or service?

The Canadian Human Rights Commission, at 1-888-214-1090.

Where do I file a human rights complaint in Ontario?

Directly with the Human Rights Tribunal of Ontario (HRTO), at 1-866-598-0322.

Is there a time limit to file?

Generally 12 months (federal) or 1 year (Ontario HRTO) from the incident — file as soon as possible, as deadlines vary by province.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.
