Frequently Asked

Where do I file a consumer complaint in Alberta?

The Service Alberta Consumer Contact Centre, at 1-877-427-4088.

Where do I file a tenancy dispute in Alberta?

The Residential Tenancy Dispute Resolution Service (RTDRS), at 310-0000 then 780-644-3000.

Where do I complain about an Alberta government department?

The Alberta Ombudsman, at 1-888-455-2756.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.

Where do I report cyber crime in Canada?

The Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre (1-888-495-8501) and your local police service. ComplaintCA's Cyber Crime module helps you document the report first.
