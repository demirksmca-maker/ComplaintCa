Frequently Asked

Where do I file a complaint against a federally regulated employer?

The Federal Labour Program, at 1-800-641-4049.

Where do I file an employment standards complaint in Ontario?

The Employment Standards Information Centre, at 1-800-531-5551.

Does this cover workplace discrimination?

No — discrimination and harassment go to a human rights body, not an employment standards office.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.
