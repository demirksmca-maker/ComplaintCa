Frequently Asked

Where do I file a consumer complaint in Nova Scotia?

Service Nova Scotia's Public Enquiries line, at 1-800-670-4357.

Where do I file a consumer complaint in New Brunswick?

The Financial and Consumer Services Commission (FCNB), at 1-866-933-2222.

Where do I file a consumer complaint in PEI?

The Financial and Consumer Services Division, at 1-800-658-1799.

Where do I file a consumer complaint in Newfoundland and Labrador?

The Consumer and Financial Services Division, at 1-877-968-2600.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.

I'm Deaf or hard of hearing — how do I call a phone-only authority?

Use Canada VRS (free sign-language video relay, available 24/7) or dial 711 for a text-based relay operator — both let you place a real phone call to any number listed on this page.
