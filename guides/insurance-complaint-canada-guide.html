How to File an Insurance Complaint in Canada

Denied claim, or a dispute over what your policy actually covers? For home, auto, and property insurance, there's a free national ombudservice — but you need one specific document from your insurer before they'll take your case. This guide walks through the real process.

Find Your Path

Step 1 — Get your insurer's final position

Work through your insurer's own complaint process first. You need their final position letter on the issue before GIO can open a case.

Step 2 — Contact GIO (free)

The General Insurance OmbudService handles claims disputes, policy coverage interpretation, and policy processing issues for home, auto, and property insurance — all free to consumers.

GIO — Submit a Complaint — 1-877-225-0446 →

Life or health insurance? Different body

GIO covers general insurance only. If your dispute is about a life insurance policy or a health/disability insurance claim, it needs a different ombudservice — ask Asya below and it'll point you to the right one.

Where ComplaintCA Fits

ComplaintCA doesn't replace GIO or your insurer's own process — it helps you draft a clear, factual complaint to your insurer, in one of 7 supported languages, so you have a solid record for your final position letter and, if needed, your GIO complaint.

Frequently Asked

Can I go straight to GIO?

No — you need a final position letter from your insurer first.

Does GIO cover life or health insurance?

No — that goes to a separate ombudservice for life and health insurance.

Is GIO free?

Yes, all GIO services are free to consumers.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.

I'm Deaf or hard of hearing — how do I call a phone-only authority?

Use Canada VRS (free sign-language video relay, available 24/7) or dial 711 for a text-based relay operator — both let you place a real phone call to any number listed on this page.
