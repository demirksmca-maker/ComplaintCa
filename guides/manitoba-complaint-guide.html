Frequently Asked

Where do I file a consumer complaint in Manitoba?

The Manitoba Consumer Protection Office, at 1-800-782-0067.

Where do I file a tenancy dispute in Manitoba?

The Residential Tenancies Branch, at 1-800-782-8403.

Where do I complain about a Manitoba government department?

The Manitoba Ombudsman, at 1-800-966-1938.

What is ComplaintCA?

A Canadian civic complaint platform that routes you to the correct authority, with AI-assisted drafting in 7 languages, an MP finder, a lawyer panel, and cyber crime reporting support.

Where do I report cyber crime in Canada?

The Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre (1-888-495-8501) and your local police service. ComplaintCA's Cyber Crime module helps you document the report first.

I'm Deaf or hard of hearing — how do I call a phone-only authority?

Use Canada VRS (free sign-language video relay, available 24/7) or dial 711 for a text-based relay operator — both let you place a real phone call to any number listed on this page.
